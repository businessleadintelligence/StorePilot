1. Executive Summary
StorePilot is an AI-powered operational intelligence platform built exclusively for Shopify merchants. It functions as an AI Chief Operating Officer — continuously monitoring store performance, detecting operational issues before they become revenue-impacting problems, and delivering actionable recommendations rather than raw data.
Unlike conventional analytics dashboards that require merchants to interpret metrics themselves, StorePilot tells merchants what is wrong, why it happened, and what to do next. The platform is designed for founder-led ecommerce brands and small teams generating between $10,000 and $500,000 per month on Shopify.
This Product Requirements Document defines the complete functional and non-functional requirements for StorePilot V1, derived exclusively from the Founder Specification v1.0.

2. Product Vision & Goals
2.1 Product Vision
StorePilot is the operational intelligence layer for ecommerce businesses — an AI COO that tells merchants what is wrong, why it happened, and what to do next.
StorePilot is explicitly not an analytics platform and not a reporting platform. It is an operational intelligence platform. The distinction is fundamental: where dashboards present data for human interpretation, StorePilot interprets the data and delivers decisions-ready intelligence.
2.2 Primary Product Goals
Goal 1
Eliminate the need for merchants to manually monitor dashboards to detect operational issues
Goal 2
Translate every detected anomaly into a business-language recommendation with estimated revenue impact
Goal 3
Build a persistent operational memory per store, making StorePilot smarter over time
Goal 4
Deliver measurable, trackable ROI so merchants can quantify the platform's value
Goal 5
Complete onboarding in under 2 minutes to minimize time-to-value
2.3 Primary USP
Stop Checking Dashboards. Your AI COO Tells You What's Wrong, Why It Happened, And What To Do Next.
2.4 Secondary USP
Detect revenue leaks, inventory risks, conversion drops, refund spikes, and growth opportunities before they impact your business.
3. Core Product Philosophy
Three non-negotiable rules govern all product decisions, AI behavior, and feature design in StorePilot.
Rule 1 — AI Explains, Not Calculates
The AI layer is responsible for explaining, interpreting, and communicating. All numerical calculations — revenue impact, health scores, anomaly thresholds — are performed by deterministic business logic. The AI must never generate or infer numbers independently.
Rule 2 — Every Recommendation Must Be Complete
No recommendation may be surfaced to a merchant unless it includes all five required components:
Problem — a clear description of the detected issue
Likely Cause — the AI's explanation of why this is happening
Estimated Revenue Impact — a monetary figure calculated by the Revenue Impact Engine
Recommended Action — a specific, actionable step the merchant can take
Confidence Score — a calibrated confidence level (Very High / High / Moderate / Low)
Rule 3 — Every Insight Must Be Tied to Business Outcomes
StorePilot does not surface vanity metrics. Every insight must connect to one of the following measurable business outcomes:
Revenue
Profitability
Inventory performance
Conversion rate
Operational efficiency

The daily AI-generated summary rendered inline. Provides the equivalent of a daily morning briefing from a COO.
8.3 Section 3 — Priority Issues
Ranked list of active operational issues, ordered by priority score (Revenue Impact x Confidence x Urgency). Each issue card displays:
Problem description
Revenue Impact (monetary)
Confidence Level
Recommended Action
8.4 Section 4 — Revenue Opportunities
Identified growth opportunities ranked by estimated revenue gain. Each card displays:
Opportunity description
Estimated Revenue Gain (monthly)
Recommended Action
8.5 Section 5 — AI COO Chat
Persistent AI chat assistant accessible from the dashboard. Context-aware and linked to the store's operational history. Provides explanations, business reasoning, recommended actions, and confidence levels in every response.
