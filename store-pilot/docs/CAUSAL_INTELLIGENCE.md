# Causal Intelligence

StorePilot transforms operational intelligence into causal reasoning.

## Question shift

| Traditional | StorePilot |
|-------------|------------|
| What changed? | Why did it change? |

## Layers

1. **Signals** — evidence fact groups, pattern seeds, baselines
2. **Correlations** — cross-domain deterministic relationships
3. **Causal chains** — auditable step-by-step cause paths
4. **Root causes** — ranked, confidence-scored outcomes
5. **Explanations** — GPT narrates structured payloads only

## Supported outcomes

Revenue decrease/increase, conversion decrease/increase, inventory shortages, refund spikes, traffic loss, SEO degradation, pricing anomalies, slow-moving products, collection underperformance, operational bottlenecks, and more.

## Future connectors

GA4, GSC, PageSpeed, Clarity plug in via normalized evidence — never direct API queries from this engine.
