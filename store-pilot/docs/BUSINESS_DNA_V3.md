# Business DNA v3

Module: `app/merchant-intelligence/business-dna/dna-v3-builder.ts`

Extends Business DNA with:

- Business characteristics (from v1/v2)
- Merchant decision style
- Optimization maturity
- Experiment maturity
- Risk tolerance
- Automation readiness
- Operational discipline
- Learning velocity
- Personalization score
- Decision consistency

Versioned in `business_dna_versions`. Incremental — new version per intelligence refresh.
